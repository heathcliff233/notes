**T13**   $f(x) = \ln(x-\sqrt{x^2-1})$
$$
y = x - \sqrt{x^2 - 1} \\
f(y)-f(y^*) = f'(y)|y - y^*| \\
= \frac{|y-y^*|}{y}  \\
= \frac{0.00005}{0.0167}  \\
= 0.3 \times 10^{-2}
$$

$$
y = x + \sqrt{x^2 -1}  \\

= \frac{|y-y^*|}{|y|}  \\
